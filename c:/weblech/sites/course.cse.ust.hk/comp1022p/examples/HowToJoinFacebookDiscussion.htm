A tutorial to join the Facebook Discussion Page of COMP1022P

 

Step 1: Registering a Facebook account

You are required to have a Facebook account. If you dont, you can register one for free (http://www.facebook.com)

Step 2: Login to the Facebook account

You need to click Login to see this content and enter the Facebook account information

You will be prompted to enter the Email and Password to login the Facebook account

After that, click Log In to proceed. The step 2 can be skipped if you have already logged in to the Facebook in your web browser.

Step 3: Access the COMP1022P Discussion Page

 

The link to the COMP1022P discussion page will be shown after you have successfully logged in to your Facebook account.

 

Click the link to access our discussion page
