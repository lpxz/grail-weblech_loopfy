COMP5622 studies advanced topics in computer and communication networks. The course focuses on the following areas:
  • Multicast
  • Peer-to-peer networking
  • Wireless and mobile networking
  • Sensor networks
  • Multimedia networking and quality of service
  • Network analysis and simulation
  • Network security
  • Traffic control
  • Cloud computing
  • Multicore and distributed systems
  • Datacenter networks
Besides learning from the textbook, students will read RFCs, present a research paper, and work on a course project in this course. Grades will be based on homework, participation (including the presentation), the project and the final exam.

Textbooks
Computer Networking: A Top-Down Approach (5th Edition), James F. Kurose and Keith W. Ross, Pearson.
