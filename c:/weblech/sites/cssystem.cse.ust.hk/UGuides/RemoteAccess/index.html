Remote Access to facilitate CSD resources

From time to time, users may wish to access the resources provided by CSD when they are working at home or being on a trip. The most frequently needed resources being the data files access. In this guideline page, we are trying to offer different solutions for this goal.

The most basic data files which can easily be accessed remotely are the files stored on UNIX file servers. E.g. User home directory, project directory. Users can SSH to anyone of the departmental UNIX workstations and access the files directly or easily copy the files around.

  • General remote access workstations
  • Displaying X application to PC runing X-Win32
  • Quick Start on SSH/SCP/SFTP

SSH can be download either from SSH.com or you may get it from here locally. This requires your CSD UNIX username/password.


However, if users store some of their files on their local PC at office and wish to retrieve any of them off-campus, this may not be that trivial and this is the main objective of having this guideline page.
