Undergraduate Laboratories

The core configuration of the machines in the teaching labs will be frozen during the semester, any special requests like software installation, parameters tuning, virtual PC image distribution, etc, should be made before semester commences. All late requests will be handled individually, and may not work perfectly as expected. For instance, distribution of softwares/images requires the TAs to help verify if it works properly at each machines.
