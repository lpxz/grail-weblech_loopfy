The ECSA Group

The ECSA (Exascale Computing System Architecture) group is part of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. Research projects in the group focus on various aspects of large scale network and high performance computing.

Rsearch projects

  • Layer Zero (L0): A general-purpose substrate for cloud computing.
  • CCMR: Cloud-computing Center for Multidisciplinary Research
    CCMR is a research cloud computing platform used by multimple research groups for scientific computing and interdisciplinary research.
