The 2011 International Workshop on Data Cloud (D-CLOUD 2011)

Final Submission Instructions

The papers in the D-CLOUD workshop will be published together with the CSC'11 publications, following the same formatting instructions for the CSC 2011 camera-ready manuscripts. If you have any questions related to publications, please contact the workshop chair. The camera-ready manuscript is due at 23:59pm Nov. 20, 2011 EDT.

Following the CSC 2011 and IEEE Xplore® guidelines, you need to complete the following three steps to prepare the camera-ready manuscript after revising the paper based on the reviews:
      1. Formatting the paper and verifying the IEEE Xplore® compliance via PDF eXpress Plus
      2. Uploading your paper via IEEE PDF eXpress Plus
      3. Submitting your copyright form

1. Formatting and IEEE Xplore® Compliance

Your accepted paper will appear on IEEE Xplore® - the IEEE Digital Library, and it is important that your submitted PDF file meets the specification for IEEE Xplore®. Failing to submit an IEEE Xplore® compatible PDF file will result in the file not being included in the publication. Please refer to the IEEE document "Simplified Requirements for Creating PDF Files for IEEE Xplore®" for more information.

The page limit for the workshop papers is 6 pages. At most two extra pages are allowed with USD100 fee for each extra page. The extra page fee needs to be paid together with the registratioon fee. Note that you should prepare the final manuscript with no page numbering.

Verification of IEEE Xplore® Compliance via PDF eXpressTM Plus

In order to assure that your submitted pdf file is IEEE Xplore® compliant, it must pass the IEEE PDF eXpressTM Plus check. The service is free of charge for CSC 2011 authors and can be accessed using the following information:

  • Go to the IEEE PDF eXpressTM plus login page
  • Click on the link "New Users - Click Here" and fill in your information. you will need the conference id (csc11xp)
  • You can find more detailed instructions on using IEEE PDF eXpressTM Plus here

You may also use IEEE PDF eXpressTM to convert your (non-PDF) manuscript into a compatible PDF file. The IEEE PDF eXpressTM can convert a wide range of formats to IEEE xplore®-compatible PDFs.

2. Submitting your Final Manuscript

Name your manuscript using the following naming convention:
<First word of title>-<second word of title>-<presenting author's last name>.pdf

For instance, if the presenting author of the paper "Extended Transactional Semantics in NoSQL Database Systems" is John Smith, the file name of the submission should be

extended-transactional-smith.pdf
Once you are satisfied with the final manuscript after it being checked by IEEE PDF eXpress TM Plus, log into your IEEE PDF eXpressTM Plus account and approve your PDF for our collection.

3. IEEE Copyright Form

Authors are required to transfer the copyright of their accepted papers to IEEE. This form is available here in: Word and PDF. Please fill out (title, author names and IEEE publication title) and sign the form, then scan it into a PDF file, and mail it to the workshop chair at dcloud2011@easychair.org.

For the IEEE Publication Title in the Copyright form, please fill in "2011 International Conference on Cloud and Service Computing."

IMPORTANT NOTES:

One workshop or conference registration must be paid for EACH paper to be published. See more registration information.

IEEE reserves the right to exclude or remove a paper from the proceedings if the paper is not presented at the conference.
