The 2011 International Workshop on Data Cloud (D-CLOUD 2011)

Special Invited Talks

Personal Cloud Storage -- Youdao Note

Dr. Weihang Jiang

Nease Inc. (163.com)

Abstract: Personal Cloud Storage is cloud computing service built around personal digital assets. The talk will cover one personal cloud storage product -- youdao note, including its server architecture, syncing, cloud+mobile strategy, and how to achieve unified cross-platform experience. The talk will also list some related research topics interesting personal cloud storage industry.

Biography: Weihang Jiang is the lead of Youdao Note product unit at Netease Inc. He got his Ph.D. in Computer Science at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, major in storage system. Before joining Netease Inc., he was working at a Silicon Valley start-up company, commercializing academic outcomes from UIUC research lab. He had also worked at NetApp and HP Lab.

Baidu Storage (Mandarin)

Zhenyu Hou

Baidu Inc.

Abstract: Supporting a diversity of Internet-scale applications, Baidu's storage infrastructure must provide reliable, high-performance, and consistent storage services for workloads ranging from web-scale indexing and searching to user generated contents. The talk will review the requirements and challenges of the storage infrastructure, and introduce several solutions developed for providing large-scale, high-throughput, real-time, and strongly consistent data storage service.

Biography: Mr. Hou Zhenyu is a senior archtect at Baidu. He received B.S. from Nankai University and received Master's degree from the Chinese Academy of Science. He joined Baidu in 2003 and have led several projects including Baidu Tieba, Baidu Zhidao, and Baidu Hi Space. He is currently taking charge of the architectural planning and design of Baidu's cloud infrastructure and the development of a super-large-scale distributed data processing platform.
