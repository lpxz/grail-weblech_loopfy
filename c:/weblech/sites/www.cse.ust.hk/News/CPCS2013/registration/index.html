Cathay Pacific Catering Services (HK) Ltd Career Talk

Event: Cathay Pacific Catering Services (HK) Ltd Career Talk

Date: 6 March 2013 (Wednesday)

Time: 2:30-3:30pm

Venue: Room 4334 (Lift 3)

Program run-down:

  • 2:30-3:05pm Company and graduate position introduction
  • 3:05-3:20pm Sharing session
  • 3:20-3:30pm Q & A session

* Leaflet and application forms will be distributed during the talk. Students are welcome to submit them after the talk.

Registration deadline: 1 March 2013 (Friday), 12:00pm

Analyst Programmer / Programmer

Responsibilities

  • Develop, implement, and support various application systems

Requirements

  • Degree holder in Computer Science, Software Engineering or related discipline
  • Knowledge in C/C++, SQL and ER/Class Diagram is essential
  • Knowledge in Powerbuilder, UNIX, VB.net, ORACLE, PL/SQL, J2EE is preferred
  • Hardworking and willing to work under pressure
  • Self-motivated, strong problem solving and analytical skills
  • Good communication skills in both English and Chinese
  • Pager support after office hour may be required
  • Candidates with less experience will be considered as Programmer
  • Fresh graduates are also welcome
