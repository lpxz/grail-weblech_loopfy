Dr. Albert Chung and Dr. Sung Kim Received the Best Ten Lecturers Awards 2010

Dr. Albert Chung and Dr. Sung Kim of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering have recently been awarded the Best Ten Lecturers Award presented by VERTEX, the House II Students Association.

This is the 14th consecutive year this event has been organized.

Congratulations, Dr. Chung and Dr. Kim!
