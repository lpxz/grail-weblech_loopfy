Chiew-Lan Tai

Professor

Chiew-Lan Tai is a Professor at the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science & Technology. She received her B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Mathematics from the University of Malaya, M.Sc. in Computer and Information Sciences from the National University of Singapore, and D.Sc. in Information Science from the University of Tokyo. Her research interests include geometric modeling and processing, computer graphics, and interactive techniques.
