Research Interests

My general goal is to help programmers produce high quality code as easy as possible. My broad research interests in the area of programming language and software engineering include:

  • Runtime monitoring
  • Concurrency defect analysis
  • Memory consistency models
  • Aspect-oriented programming
  • Middleware and distributed systems
  • Model checking and symbolic execution
  • Record/replay of nondeterministic programs
