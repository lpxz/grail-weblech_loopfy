Bio

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.

My advisor is Professor Mounir Hamdi.

I received my B.E. degree from Department of Communication Engineering, Nankai University, Tianjin, China in 2009.

Research Interests

  • Wireless Networking
  • PHY/MAC Layer Advances
  • Rateless Coding
