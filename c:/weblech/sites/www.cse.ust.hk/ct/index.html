Tip of the day

Many words sound the same or similar but don't have the same meaning - Take a look at:

Loan/lone.

Loan is a verb meaning to allow someone to borrow.

Could you loan me your car tonight?

Lone is an adjective meaning the only one, without companions.

I could see a lone figure sitting on the beach.

Welcome to the CSE Communicator Tutor (CT) Web Site!

Our job is to help you you with your written and oral communication.  We work part-time, and our services are free of charge for both undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) students.

Communication Tutors for UGs

Noorliza Daveau & Ted Spaeth help CSE students with:

  • FYP report development and copy editing
  • FYP posters
  • FYP oral presentations
  • IELM 311 investigative interview and report
  • CVs and cover letters
  • Copy editing for any piece of writing
  • Mock interviews
  • English conversation practice

For 2012-2013 FYPs, Noorliza is responsible for helping groups AR1-FL2 and GCH-JA2; Ted is responsible for helping groups FR1-FR4 and JAGCH1-YIKE3.
