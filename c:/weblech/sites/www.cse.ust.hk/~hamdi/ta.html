Teaching Awards

  • Received the Best Ten Lecturers Award (out of almost 500 faculty members) at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, 1999. This award is given once a year, and is done through University-wide voting by all students to elect the best 10 teachers of the whole University. Less than 6% of the faculty has received this prestigious award since its introduction in 1996.
