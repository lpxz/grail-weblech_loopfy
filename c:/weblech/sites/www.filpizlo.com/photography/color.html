Filip Pizlo / Photography /
Color and Tone

Colorful images of a variety of subjects. Their main draw is the composition and combination of colors, rather than the subjects they depict. Locations include Indiana, Arizona, and Poland.

These were shot with: EOS 20D, EOS 1v, Leica M4-2, Praktica MTL5b, Yashica Mat 124G, and Olympus E-P1. I don't often take images of this nature, and look back on them as a learning exercise. Some of them were an opportunity to practice focus and depth of field control, while others were an opportunity to attempt to create a nice image out of a mundane subject just by appropriately arranging the composition.
