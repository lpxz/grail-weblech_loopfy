Filip Pizlo / Photography /
Color Landscapes

I first realized that I enjoyed photography, and that I was potentially rather good at it, when I took some landscapes in Arizona, Utah, and California. Since then I became an avid landscape photographer, at least until I discovered fashion. The photos here cover a period of nine years. These days, I typically shoot one landscape image a year. The last one was taken in October 2010.

Almost half of these photos are shot on film; the rest are digital. I have not used film for landscape work since 2006. I've used a variety of equipment: a Canon 20D, Yashica Mat 124G, Leica M4-2, EOS 1v, Bessa-R, and an Olympus E-P1. About half of these are from Arizona; the rest are from California, Indiana, Washington, Kansas, Poland, and Brazil.
