Notable black and white images, featuring a variety of subjects - including landscape, street, portraiture, live performance, still life, and fashion. Many of these images were taken with a Leica M4-2; for a long time this was my main tool for black and white work. The rest were taken with a Yashica Mat 124G, EOS 1v, EOS 20D, EOS D30, Rebel XS, or a Bessa-R. Locations include California, Arizona, Washington, Indiana, Illinois, Washington D.C., and Poland.
