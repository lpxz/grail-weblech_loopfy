The Rest

Random interesting images that otherwise lack category. This includes self-portraits, pictures of friends, and pictures from various business trips. All these are fairly recent and were shot with diginal SLRs such as the EOS 20D, 30D, or 50D.

Self-portraits, as well as pictures of friends, are often my best opportunity to practice technique. Many of the lighting and processing techniques that I currently use in fashion photography originated from my attempts at taking self-portraits. Likewise, many of my post-processing techniques started out as attempts to take a random candid snapshot and turn it into something to be proud of. As such, though these images may be rather random, they represent a fairly high degree of technical sophistication.
